## Objection table

|---|---|
| "Is our data training your model?" | No. Your data never trains a shared or public model. Isolated per client, in your tenant. Slide 19 is the list. And bring your MSA. |
| "Does it replace our creatives?" | No. It removes the mechanics. Creative judgment stays with your creatives; anything bigger than copy, layout, and frames routes to them as a precise to-do (slides 14, 18). |
